Adoptions

Save 2 lives!

The best way to save a life is adoption, a little one who has nothing will become part of your life, it's a beautiful process and an admirable gesture. but before making that decision, it must be borne in mind that it will not change the life of the animal only but also that of your family, so all members must agree, and be aware of the costs and responsibilities involved in having a furry at home.

By adopting a dog or a cat you save two lives, that of your new friend and that of another abandoned one who will be able to take his place.

We say this, because unfortunately there are many abandoned animals and we do not have room for everyone.

Until one comes out, we can't rescue another.
